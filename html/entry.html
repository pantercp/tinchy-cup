Rules & Guidelines

  1. Respect each other. Bullying and name-calling will not be tolerated.
  2. Racist, sexist or abusive language is stricty prohibited.
  3. Entry fee must be paid before the FPL season begins.
  4. Gambling is not encouraged although conversations about bets are permitted.
  5. Controversial issues will be handled democratically.
  6. The Tinchy Cup competition reserves the right to ban members who break any of the rules.

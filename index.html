About The Competition

The Tinchy Cup was established to be a fun and competitive league for those who enjoy playing FPL (Fantasy Premier Leaue). The competition prides itself on being inclusive and governed democratically. All of the money raised from entrants are used to make the total cash prize pool. With the aim of making this a competitive league for everybody, we divide the money into as many prizes as appropriate so that competitors may always be in it to win it!

Month's Summary

March was an intensely competitive month, spanning only three Gameweeks, and ultimately decided by a narrow margin of just three points. In fact, a mere six points separated first place from fifth for the entire month! Nonetheless, Matt managed to emerge as the victor for the second consecutive month, securing March's coveted cash prize.

Matt's triumph can be attributed to his bold captaincy decision in GW28. Rather than playing it safe, he opted for a differential choice and selected Saka to lead his team against Crystal Palace. The move paid off in spades, as Saka scored two goals and provided an assist in Arsenal's dominant 4-1 victory.

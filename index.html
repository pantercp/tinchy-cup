About The Competition

The Tinchy Cup was established to be a fun and competitive league for those who enjoy playing FPL (Fantasy Premier Leaue). The competition prides itself on being inclusive and governed democratically. All of the money raised from entrants are used to make the total cash prize pool. With the aim of making this a competitive league for everybody, we divide the money into as many prizes as appropriate so that competitors can always be in it to win it!
